TaleSmith emerged from a desire to provide greater immersion during face-to-face roleplaying sessions. This software intends to gather on a single screen the enhanced script of the adventure, an audio manager to control music and sound effects played during the session and an intuitive system for broadcasting visuals and videos to a separate display.

It also integrates the SRD of the fifth edition and provides specific features for this system such as quick rules access, reference cards for spells and items, and an initiative tracker. Other features will be added along the way such as monsters’ hit points tracker, a markdown notepad for the gamemaster and much more !

Once upon a time, there was a group of four nerdy-ass engineers that shared the same passion for roleplaying games as for programming. One day, during a game session around the table, while fighting a huge army of gobelins, one of them realized something. “What if we added battle music, sound effects, and a visual of the battle scene ?” And from this day, our party of four adventurers set forth upon the task of creating a tool that would help gamemasters to reach a top-notch immersion. There was created The Tales Guild.

An augmented script of the adventure containing hypertext links as well as rules snippets that appear on hover. This interactiveness allows the gamemaster to quickly check the rules during the preparation of the game or during the sessions.

Allows you to broadcast artworks, videos, animations on external devices such as a second screen or a projector. This brings players a visual medium to help them better to imagine the scene.

Music and ambient sounds broadcast with a mixer. It allows multiple tracks to be superposed at different sound levels.

A sound effects control table allowing the gamemaster to play sound effects when deemed appropriate. What could be better than a blast of lightning to make your players fear a storm!

Notes Editor

A specially designed notepad to efficiently and quickly take notes of your sessions thanks to Markdown.

Full access to the SRD of the 5th edition of the world’s greatest roleplaying game : rules snippets, monster’s character sheets, initiative tracker…
